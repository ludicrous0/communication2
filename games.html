Goals

When Communicating with Anyone, There are Several Goals:

  • Convey your point or message across

  • Listen to what the other person has to say

  • Talk about serious or sensitive topics safely

  • Don't hide feelings or keep secrets from the other person


Conversing

Some Rules of Thumb for Communicating Effectively Are:

  • Maintain firm eye contact

  • Take into consideration what the other party feels or believes

  • Eliminate or avoid giving any miscommunication


Methods

There are Many Different Methods of Communication:

  • Talking to or listening to someone

  • Body language or actions

  • Facial expressions or tone of voice


Conveying

There are Several Ways to Convey Feelings and Ideas Through Speech:

  • You can show you care by expressing concern for the individual or helping them when needed

  • You can show respect by valuing the other person's thoughts, and being respectful can command respect as well

  • You can show love to someone by directly expressing an interest in them or by being honest with them

Using these tips, you can make sure that both you and the other party are feeling acknowledged and that there won't be many misunderstandings. These tips could be used when talking to family, friends, or partners, but could also be used just communicating in your daily lives. For example, you could communicate how much you want off the top with your barber, or describing where you want to be dropped off to your uber, or even just speaking to a waiter at a restaurant. Communicating is a very important life skill and mastering it will help you understand others and share your ideas as well.
